About Hampton Dental Clinic

Hampton Dental Clinic has been serving the area for more than 40 years. We are proudly independently owned and operated, and our current owner has more than a quarter century of experience in dentistry. We have 4 dentists who serve our office, assisted by 15 staff members who do their very best to take care of each patient who visits us. Together, our team has a combined 75+ years’ experience in dentistry. We emphasize ongoing education so that our staff are always up-to-date on what’s new in dentistry.

Services

Dental Exams and Teeth Cleaning

The most important thing you can do for your dental health is eat healthfully, brush and floss your teeth and have a regular dental exam and cleaning. Bring the whole family to Hampton Dental Clinic for this key service!

X-Rays and Sedation Dentistry

Hampton Dental Clinic uses a variety of methods to help us provide you with exemplary care, including digital X-rays and 3D panoramic X-rays. We also offer nitrous oxide sedation as needed for treatments, as well as hospital sedation.

Straighten Teeth with Orthodontics

Not everyone’s teeth grow in perfectly straight. Sometimes teeth need a little help settling into their ideal position, and that’s where orthodontics can help. Your jaw may be too small, or positioned so that you have an under- or overbite. If that’s the case, Hampton Dental Clinic’s dentists will assess your teeth and mouth to determine if orthodontia is the correct course of treatment.

Emergency Dentistry

You never know when an accident is going to happen. If you’ve damaged or lost a tooth, or if you’re in extreme pain, you probably have a dental emergency. Hampton Dental Clinic provides emergency dentistry to our established patients, so please call us right away to arrange treatment.
